About Us


PYI Technologies revolutionizes your commercial project or nurture your hobby with our drone services and products. Our wide product range includes flight controllers to beginner-friendly DIY kits along with providing services to various sectors from Agricultural, Construction industry, cinematography etc. With our hi-tech drones, we ensure high-quality, precise and efficient customised services with the use of best processors and hardware integrated into our drones for best experience.

Our Services

Our drones have a variety of useful features that make them stand out in comparison with other products on the market. From 4K camera to GPS navigation, each of our drones offers a wide set of benefits as well as unique design.

Agricultural drones

Farmers can boost the production with our precision smart agriculture... farming solutions for better crop and livestock management with drone services. Our drones collect high-quality data and provide high-resolution geo-referenced reflectance maps, elevation contours, DSM, and Vegetation Index in post-processing as well as assist in spraying of insecticides, seeds and manure to reduce labor cost and time. In order to quickly explore broad agricultural areas and determine where activities are required, we also supply farming photos and video data.

Survaying and Mapping

Our world-class aerial drone surveying and mapping methods save time and... money with topographic maps which are 98% accuracy. These drones are light-weight and have outstanding features in terms of toughness, dependability, and post-flight data processing.
